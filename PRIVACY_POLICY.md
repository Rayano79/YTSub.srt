# Privacy Policy for YTSub.srt

**Last Updated:** November 11, 2025

## Introduction

YTSub.srt ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your data.

## Data Collection and Storage

### Local Storage Only
YTSub.srt operates entirely on your device. All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's local storage API:

- **Subtitle Files**: SRT files you upload are stored locally on your device
- **Settings**: Your preferences (font size, opacity, etc.) are stored locally
- **Video Metadata**: Video titles and IDs are stored locally for subtitle management

### No Data Transmission
- We do **NOT** collect, transmit, or share any of your data
- We do **NOT** use analytics or tracking services
- We do **NOT** communicate with external servers
- All processing happens entirely on your device

## Permissions Used

### Storage Permission
- Used to save your subtitle files and settings locally on your device
- No data leaves your device

### ActiveTab Permission
- Used to detect the current YouTube video you're watching
- Only accessed when you interact with the extension

### Content Scripts
- The extension uses content scripts (defined in manifest.json) to display subtitles on YouTube pages
- Content scripts run automatically on YouTube watch pages (`*://www.youtube.com/watch*`)
- No additional permissions are required for content scripts as they are declared in the manifest

## Third-Party Services

YTSub.srt does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs. We do not use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage services
- External APIs (except YouTube's public oEmbed API for fetching video titles)

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your data is as secure as your browser's local storage
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the extension
- No data is transmitted over the internet

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- **Delete Data**: Uninstall the extension to remove all stored data
- **Export Data**: Your subtitle files are stored in Chrome's local storage
- **No Account Required**: We don't require any account or registration

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
